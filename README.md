# FY-2024-Sales-Performance-Profitability-Analysis
Sales dashboard project using Excel

🌍 1. Overview
: Business Context

Axon Electronics Limited is a mid-sized retail and e-commerce company based in Lagos, Nigeria, specializing in the distribution of consumer electronics and accessories such as laptops, printers, monitors, keyboards, and peripheral devices. The company operates across multiple regions, combining physical retail outlets with an online sales platform to serve a geographically diverse customer base.
As the business expanded, transaction volumes increased significantly across products, categories, and regions. However, despite the availability of large amounts of sales data, decision-making remained largely manual and fragmented. Management lacked a unified view of performance, making it difficult to assess profitability, identify trends, and respond quickly to changing market conditions.

Purpose of the Project  
The purpose of this project is to design and develop a comprehensive sales performance dashboard that consolidates transactional data into a structured analytical view. The dashboard enables the business to track key performance metrics such as revenue, cost, profit, and sales trends across different products, categories, and regions.
By transforming raw data into clear visual insights, the project supports faster and more informed decision-making across operational and strategic levels.

Expected Outcome

The implementation of this dashboard is expected to:
- Improve visibility into business performance across regions and product lines
- Support data-driven decision-making for management and stakeholders
- Enhance profitability by identifying high-performing and underperforming areas
- Enable early detection of trends and performance shifts
- Provide a scalable foundation for future analytics and reporting initiatives

⚠️ 2. Business Challenge

Prior to this project, Axon Electronics faced several operational and analytical challenges:
- Absence of a centralized reporting system, leading to fragmented and inconsistent data analysis
- Difficulty tracking monthly and year-over-year performance trends
- Limited visibility into product-level profitability and cost efficiency
- Inability to quickly compare performance across regions and categories
- Heavy reliance on manual reporting processes, which were time-consuming and prone to errors
- These challenges restricted the company’s ability to make timely, data-driven decisions and optimize business performance.

🎯 3. Rationale of the Project

This project was initiated to address the gap between data availability and actionable insight. Although the business generated large volumes of transactional data, there was no structured approach to analysing and interpreting this data effectively.

The project focuses on:
Converting raw transactional data into meaningful business insights
Enabling stakeholders to monitor performance in a consistent and structured manner
Reducing dependency on manual data analysis and spreadsheet-based reporting
Establishing a foundation for scalable analytics and continuous performance monitoring

🎯 4. Project Objectives

The key objectives of this project include:

- Analysing sales, cost, and profit trends across time periods
- Identifying top-performing products, categories, and regions
- Comparing year-over-year performance (2023 vs 2024)
- Tracking monthly revenue and sales patterns to detect seasonality
- Evaluating cost efficiency and profitability drivers across the business
- Providing a clear and interactive dashboard for stakeholder use

🗂️ 5. Dataset Description

The dataset used for this project consists of 301 transactional records, representing sales activity across multiple regions and product categories.

Data Fields

- OrderID – Unique identifier for each transaction
- Date – Transaction date
- Product – Item sold
- Category – Product category (e.g., Electronics, Accessories)
- Region – Geographic sales region
- Sales – Revenue generated per transaction
- Quantity – Number of units sold
- Cost – Cost associated with each transaction

Derived Metric
- Profit = Sales – Cost

This metric was calculated to evaluate profitability and support margin analysis across products and regions.

📊 7. Project Scope

Included in Scope
- Analysis of Sales, Cost, and Profit metrics
- Regional performance comparison
- Product and category-level sales breakdown

- KPI tracking and dashboard development

Excluded from Scope

- Marketing and acquisition channel data
- External economic or market factors affecting sales

📈 8. Interpretation (Key Insights)

- Revenue & Profitability
The business demonstrates strong revenue performance with high overall profitability, supported by a profit margin of 87%, indicating a highly favourable margin structure across most product lines. This suggests effective pricing and cost management strategies.

- Profit Margin Insight
The consistently high 87% profit margin highlights that the business operates with relatively low cost compared to revenue. While this is a positive indicator of profitability, it also presents an opportunity to reassess pricing strategies for potential market competitiveness, 
scale high-margin products to maximise revenue growth and 
ensure margins are sustainable across all categories

- Product Performance : 
Certain products, particularly accessories such as Mouse and Headphones, emerge as key revenue drivers. However, some products show higher cost-to-sales ratios, suggesting potential inefficiencies in sourcing or pricing.

- Regional Performance : 
The West and South regions contribute significantly to overall revenue, while the North region shows comparatively lower performance, indicating potential gaps in distribution, demand, or market penetration.

- Sales Trends : 
Sales exhibit noticeable monthly fluctuations, with clear spikes in specific periods. This suggests seasonal demand patterns, which can be leveraged for inventory planning and targeted promotions.

🚀 9. Recommendations
- Focus on High-Margin Products
Increase promotion and inventory allocation for products with strong profit margins, particularly within the accessories category.

- Improve Underperforming Regions
Conduct further analysis on low-performing regions (e.g., North) to identify gaps in pricing, marketing, or distribution strategy.

- Strengthen Cost Management
Review products with high cost relative to sales and explore opportunities for supplier negotiation or pricing adjustments.

- Leverage Seasonal Trends
Align inventory planning and marketing campaigns with identified peak demand periods to maximize revenue opportunities.

- Expand High-Performing Categories
Invest more resources into categories demonstrating strong and consistent performance, particularly where margins are highest.
